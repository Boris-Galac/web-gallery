IDS-327: Reverse Engineering and Production Drawings – Part Drawings and Assembly Drawings
  • Receive Machine and Reverse Engineer parts, sub-assemblies.
  • Gather relevant information on motors, pipework involved.
  • Draw Sheet Metal, Box Section, Round Bar parts individually, adding unique drawing numbers and properties.
  • Create any Sub-Assemblies required, combination of parts.
  • Create Main Assembly 3D Model with all Parts Sub-Assemblies within along with fixings.
  • Once all 3D Models are completed, provide individual drawings for manufacture, along with DXF (for laser / cnc) and PDF for general viewing.
  • Finally create a front end drawing with various views such as Front, Side, Top, Sections, Isometric, Exploded, along with clear Bill of Materials and balloon items.
